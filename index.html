  • Luxury island resort with almost no prior digital presence — no conversion tracking, no pixel, no campaign history

  • Lookalike audiences from past bookers drove cost per conversion as low as ₹6.79 on Meta
  • Google My Business optimisation + promoted pins captured map-based discovery and drove direct calls
  • Weekday offer campaigns filled off-peak periods and maintained year-round occupancy
