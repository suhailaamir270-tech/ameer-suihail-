Challenges

  • Fragrance is a sensory product — hard to sell online without scent; needed creative storytelling to drive purchase intent
  • Crowded UAE market with heavy competition from established brands on Google Search
  • Managing 4 platforms simultaneously without cannibalising audiences or inflating cost per conversion
  • Seasonal demand spikes (Ramadan, Summer) required rapid budget reallocation and creative refreshes

Strategy & Solution

  • Assigned each channel a distinct funnel role — Google for high-intent buyers, Meta for mid-funnel retargeting, TikTok & Snapchat for brand reach at lowest CPM
  • Segmented audiences by language (Arabic/English), gender (women-focused), and purchase behaviour with dedicated creatives per segment
  • Launched thematic campaigns — Ramadan Offers, Summer Sale, Perfume Combos — with dedicated landing pages and flash offers
  • Continuous creative testing: short-form video, carousel, and UGC-style content to find winning formats per channel

Challenges

  • Starting from near-zero baseline — sessions and revenue were critically low entering the campaign period
  • Competing for search traffic in a price-sensitive product category with high CPC on key terms
  • Needed simultaneous brand-building and direct-response results with a limited combined budget
  • Attribution complexity across Meta and Google — risk of double-counting and over-optimising one channel

Strategy & Solution

  • Built a full-funnel Meta architecture: video and Arabic-language campaigns warmed upper-funnel audiences at low CPM, retargeting then converted them at 3.9–5.74× ROAS
  • Google focused purely on high-intent search capture — clean campaign structure, tightly themed ad groups, strong negative keyword hygiene
  • Separated awareness spend (video viewers, Arabic campaigns) from conversion spend so budget decisions were based on clear data, not blended averages
  • Weekly performance reviews with budget reallocation toward best-performing campaigns mid-flight

Challenges

  • Extremely competitive keywords — car wrapping and PPF terms in Dubai carry high CPCs with multiple established players
  • High volume of irrelevant traffic from broad match terms inflating spend without converting
  • Lead quality was inconsistent — generating volume was not enough, leads needed to be qualified for high-ticket services
  • Meta audiences required careful exclusion management to avoid showing ads to non-car-owners and non-Dubai audiences

Strategy & Solution

  • Launched with high-volume keywords for initial data collection, then aggressively added negatives based on search query reports to eliminate irrelevant traffic
  • Restructured ad groups by service type (car wrap, PPF, vinyl, ceramic) for tighter quality scores and lower CPC
  • On Meta, built a full remarketing funnel — custom audiences (website visitors, video viewers), lookalikes from past leads, and influencer reel creatives outperforming standard ads
  • Created event-based offers and seasonal promotions on Meta to drive urgency and lower cost per lead to AED 18
